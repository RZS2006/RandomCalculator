We Are Random Calculator

We want to make the world a better place to live in and spread happiness with our high-quality technology.

Random Calculator is the world's largest and most successful calculator company. We have been empowering young people and other people of other ages for more than 100 years with our handcrafted and intricately designed calculating apparatus. Our company motto is "Whenever in doubt, give them calculators". This is forever been in our hearts to bring you what you desperately need and want. We will make the world great!

Our mission is to completly abolish the practice of using mental energy to solve tedious problems. We want our world to be stress free. We want this world to be better, happier, and more technologically advanced. Our team of 100 thousand is determined and passionate to bring you that. We also think that we should live without any sort of conflict between ourselves. With these calculators, the conflict is you not being able to decide which calculator to use.
